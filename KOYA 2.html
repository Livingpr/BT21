Koya is a blue koala with a calm and thoughtful demeanor, created by RM. He loves reading and stargazing.Koya is portrayed as a deep thinker, often pondering philosophical questions. His love for reading and stargazing highlights his introspective nature.

"Koya's sleepy but Koya's awake!" Koya often mentions his love for both sleep and staying awake to ponder.
