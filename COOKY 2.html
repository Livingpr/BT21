Cooky (Jungkook)

COOCKY

Cooky is a pink, muscular bunny created by Jungkook. He's competitive and always up for a challenge. Cooky has a competitive spirit and is often portrayed as tough and determined, despite his cute appearance. He's determined to prove he's strong like a bunny.

"I'm not a rabbit; I'm a tough, muscular bunny!" Cooky often emphasizes his strong and competitive nature.
