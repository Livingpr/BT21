Shooky (Suga)

SHOOKY

Shooky is a chocolate chip cookie with a mischievous personality, created by Suga. He often gets into funny situations. Shooky is mischievous and confident, never letting his small size hold him back. The character's cookie form represents a playful twist.

"I may be small, but I can do it!" Shooky's catchphrase showcases his determination despite his size.
